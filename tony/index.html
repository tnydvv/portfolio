About Me

As a self-taught software engineer, I am driven by a commitment to continuous learning and a passion for developing robust and efficient solutions. My professional journey includes three years of entrepreneurial experience as an owner-operator in the demanding environment of the Long Beach ports, where I managed and ran a small trucking company. Seeking a career transition, I am dedicated to leveraging my analytical and problem-solving skills in the field of software development.

My motivation is deeply rooted in my desire to be a positive role model for my family. My interest in coding was sparked by an unexpected source: the animated film "Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs". Seeing something being created from nothing truly intrigued me, and that's how I found my path to software development.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I enjoy a diverse range of interests, including film, theatre, outdoor activities like skateboarding and hiking, and being a lifelong learner. I am eager to apply my unique blend of entrepreneurial experience, creative problem-solving, and dedication to continuous learning to a challenging role in software development.
